Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm water
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/2 cup granualted sugar
  • 1 egg plus one egg yolk
  • 3 cup bread flour
  • 1/2 butter

For the filling

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/4 table spoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk wit yeast, sugar
  2. Melt the butter and add to mixture
  3. Add in flour and mix until combine in dough
